Art in the South
Anyone who has traveled only a little in the South has become con
vinced that we Southern people are not artistic as a group . The broken
down scenes on the farms , the aimless , trash - burdened streets of the
towns , the forsaken appearance of houses and yards all cry in impressive
language that beauty , symmetry and proportion are not appreciated .
Not only are there many outward evidences of the lack of art culture
here in the South , but the character of the people also spells artlessness .
