The Source of Our Colors
During the spring of 1892 , three enthusiastic young teachers had a
brilliant idea that it would be very encouraging to the fellows for the
students and teachers to wave pennants at a certain game . Not know
ing the college colors , one of the teachers asked a crowd of fellows who
were standing near . The men immediately dropped their heads , because
they had no college colors .
One of the students , the present teacher , Professor Towns , suggest
ed steel gray and crimson as a good combination , because he had got the
idea from Boston Tech . where he expected to do future study . This idea
was immediately approved by the three teachers , who were Miss Evelyn
White , now Mrs. George Knolte , Miss Jean Stenabaugh and Miss Ger
trude Dunstan . These young ladies at once made a little steel gray pen
nant and embroidered it with two old English letters , A. U. This little
emblem is still preserved in the Bursar's office and is revered by all the
student body , as the first crimson and gray .
